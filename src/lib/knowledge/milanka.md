# Milanka — Early Years Agent

Named after **Milanka Mandic**, an educator at Raul's campus high school. As students were minors living on campus, they had dedicated educators responsible for their upbringing — Milanka was one of them. Raul didn't like her much at the time. With years and distance, he understood what she did for him: a sturdy, demanding rearing that quietly shaped who he became.

## Origins

- Born in Romania
- Raised going back and forth between Romania and Serbia
- This dual upbringing is a defining part of his identity

## Identity shaped by movement

"Shaped by both, claimed by neither" — Raul doesn't identify firmly as Romanian or Serbian. He was formed by the movement between the two countries and cultures, giving him a perspective comfortable with complexity and ambiguity.

## Campus high school

Raul attended a campus high school (boarding school) named Nikola Tesla where he lived as a minor. Dedicated educators like Milanka looked after the students in place of parents. It was a formative environment — structured, demanding, and in retrospect, valuable.

## The gift of demanding people

Milanka is the kind of person you understand only later. The strictness that felt like opposition turned out to be investment. Raul's ability to recognise that — to revise his judgement of someone across decades — says something about how he thinks.

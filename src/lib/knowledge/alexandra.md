# Alexandra — Travel Agent

Named after **Alexandra Barzan**, Raul's wife. Alexandra taught him what travel can bring to a person's life. He is forever grateful and cherishes their adventures together. Her name on the travel agent is a tribute.

## Places lived

- **Romania** — birthplace; part of his upbringing
- **Serbia** — raised going back and forth between Romania and Serbia during childhood and youth
- **South of France (Côte d'Azur)** — moved here in 2018; current home

## Identity and place

His dual upbringing between two countries gave him a particular relationship with belonging: "shaped by both, claimed by neither." Neither fully Romanian nor fully Serbian in how he identifies — a person formed by movement between places.

The Côte d'Azur is clearly a place he loves. He references the light and pace approvingly ("The sun is patient"). Living on the Mediterranean since 2018 has been a sustained choice.

## What travel brought

Through Alexandra and their shared adventures, Raul came to understand travel not just as displacement but as a lens — a way to see your own assumptions from the outside. He holds those adventures with deep gratitude.
